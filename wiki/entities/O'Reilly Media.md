# O'Reilly Media

**What**: American technology-focused publisher and learning platform, founded by Tim O'Reilly. Known for its animal-cover technical books and its online learning service (formerly Safari Books Online).

In this vault, O'Reilly publishes two sources: [[Data Science from Scratch]] by [[Joel Grus]] (2015; cover: a Rock Ptarmigan) and [[Designing Data-Intensive Applications]] by [[Martin Kleppmann]] (2017; cover: a wild boar).

## Why it matters here

- It is the **provenance / publisher** for the DSFS source.
- Distinct from [[Springer]], which publishes the more academic [[Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning]] — a small signal of the two books' different registers (practitioner press vs. academic series). See [[DSFS vs PRML]].
