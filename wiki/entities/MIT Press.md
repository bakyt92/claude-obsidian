# MIT Press

**What**: The university press of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, publishing scholarly and academic works in science, technology, and the arts.

In this vault, MIT Press is the publisher of [[Deep Learning (Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville)|*Deep Learning*]] (2016), the foundational deep-learning textbook.

## Why it matters here

- **Provenance / publisher** for the Goodfellow source.
- An academic press (cf. [[Springer]] for PRML), versus the practitioner press [[O'Reilly Media]] (DSFS) — a small signal of the text's rigorous, theory-first register.
- Institutionally adjacent to [[MIT OpenCourseWare]] (both MIT), though distinct organizations.
