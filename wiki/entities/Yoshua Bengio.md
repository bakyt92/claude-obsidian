# Yoshua Bengio

**Role**: Professor at the University of Montreal; founder and scientific director of **Mila** (Quebec AI Institute); one of the most-cited researchers in deep learning.

Yoshua Bengio is a co-author of [[Deep Learning (Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville)|*Deep Learning*]] and a **2018 ACM Turing Award** laureate (shared with Geoffrey Hinton and Yann LeCun) "for conceptual and engineering breakthroughs that made deep neural networks a critical component of computing."

## Why he matters

- One of the "godfathers of deep learning"; foundational work on neural language models, word embeddings, attention mechanisms, and the difficulty of training [[Recurrent Neural Network|RNNs]] (vanishing gradients).
- Doctoral advisor of [[Ian Goodfellow]].
- In later years, a prominent voice on AI safety and governance.
