# Ian Goodfellow

**Role**: Machine-learning researcher; lead author of the *Deep Learning* textbook.

Ian Goodfellow is best known for **inventing the [[Generative Adversarial Network|Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)]]** in 2014, and as lead author of [[Deep Learning (Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville)|*Deep Learning*]] (with [[Yoshua Bengio]] and Aaron Courville). He did his PhD under [[Yoshua Bengio]] at the University of Montreal.

## Why he matters

- **GANs**: his adversarial-training idea — a generator and discriminator competing — became one of the most influential generative-modeling frameworks of the 2010s. See [[Generative Adversarial Network]].
- **The textbook**: wrote much of *Deep Learning* while at Google Brain; it became the field's standard graduate reference.
- Held senior research roles at Google Brain, OpenAI, and Apple (Special Projects), among others.
