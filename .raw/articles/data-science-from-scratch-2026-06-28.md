# Data Science from Scratch: First Principles with Python

**Author**: Joel Grus
**Publisher**: O'Reilly Media, April 2015 (First Edition)
**ISBN**: 978-1-491-90142-7 · 330 pages · Python 2.7

## Premise (from the preface / back cover)

Most data science is done with libraries (NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn) that let you
use algorithms without understanding them. This book instead *implements the
fundamental tools and algorithms from scratch, by hand, in pure Python*, so the
reader understands how they actually work. Data science sits at the intersection of
hacking skills, math/statistics knowledge, and substantive expertise; the book
focuses on the first two.
